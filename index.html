Buda or Pest?

Short answer: stay in Pest. Buda is the pretty, hilly, historic side (castle, views) — but Pest is where the food, drinks, culture and walkable energy are. Here are the best neighbourhoods.

Baths, parks & nature

Steaming thermal pools, a fairy-tale castle on a lake, a green island in the Danube, and a half-day escape up into the Buda Hills.

03

Eat & drink well

Aesthetic, tasty and not tourist traps. July is peak season — book dinner ahead!

Ruin bars, rooftop cocktails, a Ferris wheel and the magic of the lit-up Danube from the water.
